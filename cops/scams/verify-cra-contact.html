How to confirm it's the CRA calling you

If a CRA employee calls you, they will identify themselves by giving you their:

  • Name
  • Call back phone number

When a CRA employee calls you, it will show as No Caller ID.

Steps to verify it’s really the CRA calling:

  1. Do not provide any personal information to the caller
  2. Make note of their:
    • Name
    • Office location
    • Call back phone number
  3. Let them know you will verify their information
  4. End the call
  5. Do not call the number shown in the caller ID or in your call log
  6. Use the [Verify a CRA phone number component]
