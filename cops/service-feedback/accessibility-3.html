What is accessibility feedback

Accessibility feedback may be a complaint, suggestion or compliment about the accessibility of CRA programs and services.

The CRA's commitment to accessibility includes removing or addressing physical, architectural, technological or attitudinal barriers that hinder the full and equal participation in society of persons with an impairment. Impairments include physical, mental, intellectual, cognitive, learning, communication or sensory impairments or functional limitations.

Your feedback can help us make our programs and services more accessible. We welcome:

  • Feedback on barriers that you experience when dealing with the CRA
  • Feedback that will help inform our accessibility plan

If your feedback is about a particular issue you have experienced, make sure to be descriptive and include details such as the date, the platform, and the name of the webpage, application or service involved.

For feedback that is not related to accessibility, go to: send feedback about CRA service.

Submit accessibility feedback

Select a method below to submit feedback about an accessibility barrier or the Accessibility Plan:

Submit by phone

How to submit feedback by phone

You can call us using one of the following numbers:

  • From any province in Canada: 1-800-959-8281
  • From Yukon, Northwest Territories or Nunavut: 1-866-426-1527
  • From outside Canada and the U.S. call collect: 1-613-940-8495

Tell the call centre agent you have feedback about an accessibility barrier or the Accessibility Plan.

They will collect your feedback and share it with the appropriate area to be addressed.

If you prefer to give feedback anonymously, you do not need to identify yourself.

Submit by teletypewriter (TTY)

How to submit feedback by TTY

To send accessibility feedback using TTY, call: 1-800-665-0354

If you use an operator-assisted relay service, call our regular telephone numbers instead of the TTY number (refer to the Submit by phone section above for these numbers).

If you need help navigating CRA's online applications, contact us using the E-service Helpdesk TTY: 1-888-768-0951.

Submit the online form

How to submit feedback using the online form

Use the online form to submit your accessibility feedback electronically. The online form lets you complete and submit your feedback all in one place.

Submit accessibility feedback

To submit anonymous feedback, indicate that your feedback is a suggestion or a compliment. This way you do not need to identify yourself.

Send an email

How to submit feedback by email

To email accessibility feedback, select the link below to open a new email and compose your message:

CRA Service Feedback at Accessibility Service Feedback / Rétroaction sur l'accessibilité des services (CRA/ARC).

Do not email confidential or taxpayer information

Email does not meet the security requirements needed to ensure that confidential information is fully protected. Do not include any confidential information (such as, a social insurance number or tax return information) in your feedback.

We strongly recommend that you send your feedback using another method, such as the online form.

Submit by fax

How to submit feedback by fax

To send accessibility feedback by fax, write a letter or use the PDF version of the RC193, Service Feedback form.

  1. Download the RC193, Service Feedback PDF
  2. Complete and print the PDF form
  3. Fax your feedback:
    • 1-866-388-7371 from Canada or the United States
    • 1-819-536-0701 from outside Canada and the United States

Due to security and privacy concerns, we are not able to acknowledge receipt of your fax or reply by fax.

Use CRA sign-in services

How to submit feedback using CRA sign-in services

To send accessibility feedback using CRA sign-in services, you must use the PDF version of the RC193, Service Feedback form.

  1. Before you sign in, download the RC193, Service Feedback PDF
  2. Complete the PDF form and save it or scan it
  3. Sign in to your secure CRA account:
    • My Account
    • My Business Account
    • Represent a Client
  4. Using the Submit documents service, select "Collections, Relief and Feedback" from the list to submit your form (or scanned letter)

You cannot submit anonymous feedback using your secure CRA account at this time.

Send by mail

How to submit feedback by mail

To send accessibility feedback by mail, write a letter or use the PDF version of the RC193, Service Feedback form.

  1. Download the RC193, Service Feedback PDF
  2. Complete and print the PDF form
  3. Mail your feedback to:

    CRA Service Feedback
    National Intake Centre
    4695 Shawinigan-Sud Boulevard
    Shawinigan QC G9P 5H9
    CANADA

To submit anonymous feedback, do not complete sections 1 or 2 of Form RC193 or include any identifying information in your letter. We are not able to acknowledge or respond to anonymous feedback.

After you submit accessibility feedback

How we address your feedback

The CRA will acknowledge and address your accessibility feedback using the same contact method that you used to submit your feedback.

We aim to contact you within 3 to 4 weeks of receiving your feedback. We will not contact you if your feedback was anonymous.

On-going improvements

Some feedback may be addressed right away, and some may be addressed as part of our on-going accessibility improvements.

The feedback we receive and how we take it into consideration will be included in the CRA accessibility progress reports.
