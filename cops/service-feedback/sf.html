Types of feedback

There are 3 type of feedback you can give the CRA.

Compliment: Option 1 of X

Submit a positive experience with the CRA

Suggestion: Option 2 of X

Suggest an improvement for the CRA

Complaint: Option 3 of X

Submit a complaint about the CRA

Feedback about service you received

You can provide feedback on service you received from the CRA.

Options

  • By phone - Call the CRA
  • By form - Complete the form
  • By contacting the Taxpayers' Ombudsperson

Methods

  • Online dynamic form
  • Fillable PDF form RC193

Ways to submit your feedback

  • Online dynamic form
  • Online CRA account - (My account, My Bus, Rep a client)
  • By fax
  • By mail

The CRA service standard is to resolve the complaint within 30 business days of receiving your complaint.

For details: Check CRA processing times

Non-service feedback

These are other types of complaints or problems not related to service.

Some examples:

  • Disagree with or not understanding a letter, notice, payment, or other document received from the CRA
  • Dispute an assessment, determination, or decision made by the CRA
  • Disagree with the Income Tax Act, or tax legislation matters

Your rights

You can expect to be treated fairly and get a high level of service each time you deal with the CRA. All information you give to the CRA will be treated as confidential under the Privacy Act.

For details: Taxpayer Bill of Rights
