How to submit feedback using a/your secure CRA account

  1. Open the PDF Form RC193, Service Feedback or write us a letter
  2. Complete and save the form or scan it
  3. Gather any supporting documents (would we say scan them or create electronic versions?)
  4. Choose a secure account (use submit documents):

    • My Business Account
    • Represent a Client
  5. Select the submit documents option
  6. Attach your completed form and any supporting documents
  7. Submit
Mail

How to submit feedback by mail

  1. Open the PDF Form RC193, Service Feedback or write us a letter
  2. Complete and print the form
  3. Gather and include any supporting documents (optional)
  4. Mail your feedback to the CRA:

    CRA Service Feedback
    National Intake Centre
    4695 Shawinigan-Sud Boulevard
    Shawinigan QC  G9P 5H9
    CANADA

Fax

How to submit feedback by fax

  1. Open the PDF Form RC193, Service Feedback or write us a letter
  2. Complete and print the form
  3. Gather any supporting documents (optional)
  4. Fax it to the CRA:
    • 1-866-388-7371 from Canada or the United States.
    • 1-819-536-0701 from outside Canada and the United States.
