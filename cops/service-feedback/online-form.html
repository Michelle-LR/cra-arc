Service feedback - RC193 online form

Use the Service Feedback – RC193 online form to send feedback about your service experience with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

Your feedback may be about:

  • a service complaint, suggestion, or compliment
  • an accessibility barrier or the CRA Accessibility Plan

Know your taxpayer service rights

There are 16 taxpayer rights, 8 relate to service:

  • You have the right to be treated professionally, courteously, and fairly
  • You have the right to complete, accurate, clear, and timely information
  • You have the right to lodge a service complaint and to be provided with an explanation of our findings
  • You have the right to have the costs of compliance taken into account when administering tax legislation
  • You have the right to expect us to be accountable
  • You have the right to expect us to publish our service standards and report annually
  • You have the right to expect us to warn you about questionable tax schemes in a timely manner
  • You have the right to represented by a person of your choice

For details: Taxpayer Bill of Rights

The online form cannot accept additional supporting documents. If we need any additional information, we will ask for it when we contact you.

The form is not available from 3 am to 6 am ET due to daily system maintenance.

After you submit the form, print or save a copy of the confirmation page for your records.
