Free tax clinics

Through the Community Volunteer Income Tax Program (CVITP), community organizations host free tax clinics where volunteers complete tax returns for people with a modest income and a simple tax situation.

Find out how many organizations and volunteers participated in free tax clinics each year and see how many people they helped, the number of returns filed and the amount of refunds, benefit, and credits distributed.

These statistics are for the free tax clinics that are part of the Community Volunteer Income Tax Program and provide the number of:

  • community organizations

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) collects and processes this data for each calendar year.
