Free tax clinics

Through the Community Volunteer Income Tax Program (CVITP), community organizations host free tax clinics where volunteers complete tax returns for people with a modest income and a simple tax situation.

    How to get your taxes done at a free tax clinic

      • Over 649,000 individuals were helped
      • Over 735,000 returns being filed
      • Over $1.7B in refund, credit and benefit entitlements were paid out by the CRA
