Automatic Exchange of Information (AEOI) – CRS and FATCA

The information on this page is meant to help Canadian financial institutions (FIs) and their account holders understand their responsibilities in the automatic exchange of financial account information with foreign jurisdictions.

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) is responsible for collecting financial account information and exchanging it with foreign jurisdictions. This is part of an ongoing global effort to combat tax evasion, encourage voluntary compliance and ensure a fair tax system for all. Canada participates in two automatic exchanges of financial account information:

  • Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA)
  • Common Reporting Standard (CRS)
