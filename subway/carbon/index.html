Canada Carbon Rebate (CCR)

Previously known as: Climate action incentive payment (CAIP)

File early to receive your April payment on time

To receive your payment on April 15, 2024, you and your spouse or common-law partner (if applicable), must have had your income tax and benefit return filed/assessed on or before March 15, 2024. If your tax returns are assessed after this date, your payment will be included in a subsequent payment.

The Canada Carbon Rebate (CCR) is a tax-free amount to help eligible individuals and families offset the cost of the federal pollution pricing. It consists of a basic amount and a supplement for residents of small and rural communities.

During 2023 and previous years, the CCR was known as the Climate action incentive payment (CAIP).
