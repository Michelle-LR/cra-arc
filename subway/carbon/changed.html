What has changed

Staring in 2024, the Canada Carbon Rebate (CCR) is a tax-free payment to help eligible individuals and families offset the cost of the federal pollution pricing. It consists of a basic amount and a supplement for residents of small and rural communities.

  • In 2021-2023: It was known as the Climate action incentive payment (CAIP)
  • In 2018-2020: It was known as the Climate action incentive (CAI), which was a refundable tax credit claimed annually on personal income tax returns
    How to claim the CAI

    If you are looking for Climate Action Incentive credit information claimed on a tax return for the 2018 to 2020 tax years, please visit Tax packages for all years. You will need to select the appropriate year and province, then look for information related to Schedule 14 – Climate action incentive.
