File Information Returns Electronically

what to fill out

Information returns contain slips and a summary. Depending on the type of information, you may have to issue more than one type of return.

What are the benefits of filing electronically?

Filing electronically lets you:

  • get confirmation within minutes
  • file securely
  • make changes
  • allow another authirized source, such as a tax preparer, to file for you
  • send returns for the previous ten years

Types

Select the type of information return to find out who must fill out, what to report and when to issue slips.

  • NR4, Statement of Amounts Paid or Credited to Non-Residents of Canada
  • T3, Statement of Trusts Income Allocations and Designations
  • T3GR, Group Income Tax and Information Return for RRSP, RRIF, RESP, or RDSP Trusts
  • T3P, Employees’ Pension Plan Income Tax Return
