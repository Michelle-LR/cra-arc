Contact us

  • For information or assistance on filling an information return electronically, call the Electronic Media Processing Unit at 1-800-665-5164, between 7:00 and 17:00 (EST). If you are outside of Canada, call 613-940-8599.
  • For information about completing slips and summaries see Filling out slips and summaries or call 1-800-959-5525. If you are outside of Canada, call 613-940-8497.

To get email updates about electronic filing, see Electronic Filing for Businesses.

Electronic mailing list

This list will provide filers of information returns such as T4 and T5 returns with email updates about important new information on our website, including:

  • Legislative changes affecting the filing of returns, and
  • New technical specifications for filing returns electronically using the Internet (Filing Information Returns Electronically).
