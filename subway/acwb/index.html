Canada Workers Benefit

The Canada Workers Benefit (CWB) is a refundable tax credit to help individuals and families who are working and earning a low income.

The CWB has two parts: a basic amount and a disability supplement.

You can claim the CWB when you file your income tax return.

Starting in July 2023 and based on the 2022 taxation year, the Canada Workers Benefitwill provide advance payments equivalent to 50% of the CWB across 3 quarterly payments. The remaining amount will be paid during the following tax season. This initiative puts more money in the workers' pockets to help cope with the rising cost of living.

Payment dates for this year

This year, the first quarterly payment will be issued on July 28, 2023. The two remaining quarterly payments will be issued on October 12, 2023 and January 12, 2024.
