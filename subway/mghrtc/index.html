Multigenerational home renovation tax credit (MHRTC)

Status: Available starting on 2023 tax returns

The new Multigenerational home renovation tax credit (MHRTC) is a refundable tax credit that can be claimed on your individual (T1) tax return.

If you are eligible, you can claim this refundable tax credit for certain renovation expenses involved in creating a self-contained secondary unit. The secondary unit must enable a senior or an adult who is eligible for the disability tax credit to live with a qualifying relative.

The credit is 15% of the eligible renovation expenses, to a maximum of $50,000. The maximum MHRTC is $7,500.
