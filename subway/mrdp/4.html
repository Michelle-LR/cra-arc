If you are a buyer

If you are buying products or services using a digital platform, the MRDP does not apply to you. You do not have to report or file anything. Your browsing and buying information is not shared with the CRA.

If you sell products or services

Your role in the MRDP is done once you have done both of the following:

  • Determined you are a reportable seller
  • Shared the necessary information with the platform operator

However, you may still need to report any earnings and may also have additional tax obligitions with the CRA. Refer to: Taxes and the platform economy
