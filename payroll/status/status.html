This guidance does not replace the law and does not replace a formal request for a CPP/EI ruling. No changes were made to the existing federal legislative requirements.

If you are not sure if the worker is an employee or self-employed for CPP/EI purposes

Request a ruling from CRA

You need to consider multiple factors to determine if the worker is an employee or is self-employed. You can use the CRA guidance to help you gather the facts based on your situation.

If you are still not sure if the worker is an employee or self-employed after following the CRA guidance, you can ask for a CPP/EI ruling.
