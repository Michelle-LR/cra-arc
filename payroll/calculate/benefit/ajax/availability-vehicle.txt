<p>A vehicle is available to your employee if they have access to or control over the vehicle. This includes any part of a day, weekends and holidays during the calendar year.</p>

<p>If you made a vehicle available to your employee and the benefit is taxable, your employee <strong>must</strong> keep a logbook or daily record of the trips made with the vehicle. Step 6 provides more information about the <a href="#log" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">required records&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a> (logbook) your employee must keep if you determine the benefit is taxable.</p>
