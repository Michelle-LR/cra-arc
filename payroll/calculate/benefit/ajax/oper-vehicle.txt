<p>When you (or a person related to you) provide an automobile to an employee and pay for the operating expenses related to personal use (including the GST/HST and PST), this payment is a taxable benefit to the employee. </p>
<p>If the conditions met in step 12 are met, you can calculate the benefit using an optional calculation. Otherwise, the benefit is calculated using the fixed rate.</p>
