
<p>If you <a href="#availability1" aria-controls="mid-screen" class="overlay-lnk" role="button">make a vehicle available&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a> to your employee, they must keep a logbook or daily record of the trips made with the vehicle.</p>
<p> The logbook or daily record will support your calculation of the vehicle benefit and any expenses your employee will claim. <strong>They must give you a copy.</strong></p>

<details><summary><span class="fas fa-folder-open fa-lg"></span> Logbook - Detailed records</summary>
<div id="ajax-log">
<p>Your employee should record the following information in a logbook or daily record:</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-and cnjnctn-sm">
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Total number of days that the vehicle was made available to them during the year </li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Total number of kilometres travelled for business and personal driving (on a daily, weekly or monthly basis) during the number of days that the vehicle was made available </li>
</ul>

<p>Additionally, the logbook may contain the following information:</p>

<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-and cnjnctn-sm">
<li class="cnjnctn-col"><h5 class="text-muted"><a href="#personal1" aria-controls="mid-screen" class="overlay-lnk" role="button">Personal driving&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></h5>
<p>If the vehicle is used only for personal driving, your employee should record the following information:</p>
<ul class="fa-ul">
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg fa-li text-success"></span> Total number of days that the vehicle is made available to your employee (or to a person not dealing at armâ€™s length with your employee) during the year </li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg fa-li text-success"></span> Kilometres driven by the employee during each period when the vehicle is made available to your employee </li>
</ul></li>

<li class="cnjnctn-col"><h5 class="text-muted"><a href="#personal1" aria-controls="mid-screen" class="overlay-lnk" role="button">Business driving&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></h5>
<p id="doc">Your employee should record, on a daily basis, for each trip made to perform their duties, the following information:</p>
<ul class="fa-ul">
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg fa-li text-success"></span> Place of departure and the place of destination </li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg fa-li text-success"></span> Number of kilometres travelled between those two places </li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg fa-li text-success"></span> Any other information to establish that the trip is made by your employee to perform their duties </li>
</ul></li>
</ul></div>
</details>
