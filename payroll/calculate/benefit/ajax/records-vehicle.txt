
<p> The logbook or daily record will support your calculation of the vehicle benefit and any expenses your employee will claim. <strong>They must give you a copy.</strong></p>

<details><summary><span class="fas fa-folder-open fa-lg"></span> Logbook - Detailed records</summary>
<div id="ajax-log">
<p>Your employee should record the details of the use of the vehicle, such as the following information in a logbook or daily record:</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-and cnjnctn-sm">
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Total number of days that the vehicle was made available to them during the year </li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Total number of kilometres travelled for <a href="#personal1" aria-controls="mid-screen" class="overlay-lnk" role="button">business (employment-related) and personal driving&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a> (on a daily, weekly or monthly basis) during the number of days that the vehicle was made available </li>
</ul>
