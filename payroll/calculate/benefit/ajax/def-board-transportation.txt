
<details><summary>Examples: Board and lodging benefit vs housing or utilities benefit</summary>

<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or cnjnctn-sm">
<li class="cnjnctn-col"><h4>Board and lodging benefit:</h4>
<ul>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg text-success"></span> Generally provided to your employees along with salary</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg text-success"></span> May include meals or travel expenses, travel expenses includes: 
<ul>
 <li>Cost of airplane</li>
     <li>Train or bus tickets</li>
     <li>Expenses related to the use of a vehicle</li>
     <li>Cost of meals, board and lodging</li>
     <li>Incidental expenses such as taxi fares, tolls and ferry charges </li>
</ul></li>

<p class="mrgn-tp-lg">Learn more for specific board and lodging rules if: <a href="remote.html">your employee works in a remote location</a> 
or if <a href="special-site.html">your employee works at a special work site, including in a prescribed zone</a> </p>
</ul></li>

<li class="cnjnctn-col"><h4>Housing or utilities benefit (allowance):</h4> 
<ul>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg text-success"></span> Accommodation you provide to an employee rent-free, or for rent that is below the fair market value a house, accommodation includes:
<ul>
 <li>House</li>
<li>Condominium</li>
<li>Apartment</li>
<li>Room</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg text-success"></span> Allowances towards housing costs you pay your employee</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-check fa-lg text-success"></span> May include the cost of domestic utilities, utilities expenses includes:
<ul>
 <li>Electricity</li>
<li>Natural gas</li>
<li>Water</li>
<li>Telephone</li>
<li>Internet</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><span class="fas fa-ban text-danger fa-lg"></span> Generally, it <strong>does not</strong> include meals and transportation</li>

<p class="mrgn-tp-lg">Learn more for specific housing rules if: <a href="clergy.html">your employee is a member of the clergy or of a religious order</a></p>
</ul></li>


</ul>
</details>
