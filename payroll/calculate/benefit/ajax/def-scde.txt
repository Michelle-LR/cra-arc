<details><summary>What is a SCDE</summary>
<p>A principal place of residence is considered a self-contained domestic establishment (SCDE) if the following apply: </p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-and">
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">A dwelling-house, apartment, or other similar place of residence in which place a person as a general rule sleeps and eats  </li>
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">Generally a living unit with restricted access that contains a kitchen, bathroom, and sleeping facilities</li>
  <li class="cnjnctn-col">Separate from any other living unit in the same building.</li>
</ul>
<p class="mrgn-bttm-md"><span class="fas fa-times text-danger fa-lg"></span> Generally, a room in a hotel, dormitory, boarding house, or bunkhouse is not considered a SCDE.  </p>
</details>
