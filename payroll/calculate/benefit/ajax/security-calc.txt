
<h3>Events that cause the employee benefit</h3>
<div id="ajax-calc0"> 
<details><summary>How to calculate a foreign plan if you are a non-resident employer</summary>
<p>When calculating the taxable benefit, the foreign stock options must be converted to the Canadian dollar equivalent. </p>
<p>You can use the exchange rate (available from the company or the Bank of Canada) in effect on the dates that make up the transaction (acquisition date): the date the shares are paid for and the date when the treasury shares have been transferred to your employee. </p>
<p>Where the amount paid or to be paid by an employee pursuant to the terms of a stock option agreement is expressed in terms of a foreign currency, it will not compromise your employee's eligibility for the deduction under paragraph 110(1)(d).</p>
</details>
