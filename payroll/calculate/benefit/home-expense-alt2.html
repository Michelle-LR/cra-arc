Determine if a benefit is taxable

Generally, if you provide a benefit to your employee to compensate for use of their home or for home office expenses in connection with or in the course of their office or employment duties, the benefit is taxable. Depending on the situation, the benefit may not be taxable under the new CRA's administrative policy .

Under the CRA's new administrative policy, the benefit is not taxable if all of the following conditions apply:

If you do not meet all conditions above, the benefit is taxable.
