  <li>  <h3 id="step2">Determine the intent of the parties in forming the agreement</h3>

 <li>  <h3 id="step3">Review the meaning of a contract of employment under the Civil Code of Québec</h3>
 <p>Once you completed your <a href="#ajax-contract" aria-controls="ajax-contract" class="overlay-lnk" role="button">review of the contract <span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a> to determine the intent of the parties, you need to review the definition of a contract of employment under the Civil Code of Québec (C.C.Q.).</p>

<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or">
<li class="cnjnctn-col"><h4 class="text-muted">Factors to consider: Employee</h4>
<p> For the worker to be considered an employee, the facts of their working relationship with the payer <strong>must match</strong> the following 3 elements of that <abbr title="Civil Code of Quebec">C.C.Q.</abbr> definition:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="#control" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">Carrying out the work&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></li>
<li><a href="#remuneration" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">Remuneration&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></li>
<li><a href="#subordination" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">Relationship of subordination&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col"><h4 class="text-muted">Factors to consider: Self-employed worker</h4>
<p>For the worker to be considered self-employed (contract of enterprise or for services), the key elements are different. The most important difference is that it <strong>does not</strong> have a <a href="#subordination" aria-controls="centred-popup" class="wb-lbx" role="button">relationship of subordination&nbsp;<span class="far fa-question-circle"></span></a></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>

  <li>  <h3 id="step4">Gather all facts to determine the status of the worker</h3>
<p>Once you completed your review of the meaning of employment under the <abbr title="Civil Code of Quebec">C.C.Q.</abbr>, you need to gather all the facts to help you determine the status of the worker. </p>
<div data-ajax-replace="/cra-arc/payroll/emp-status/factors.html#ajax-step3"></div>
 <ol class="lst-stps-sub ld-zr">

            </div>
          </div>

      </div>

          </div>

      </div>

            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>
    </li>
<li><h4 id="factor3">Carrying out the work</h4>
<div id="ajax-control"> 
      <p id="3_2">Control over where, when and how the work is done needs to be considered when determining the degree of control held by the payer or the degree of independence held by the worker. The actual degree of control will vary with the type of work and the skills of the worker.</p>
 </div>
      <p class="h5" id="f3q01">Q5. Did the worker’s position have a job description in the payer’s internal policies?</p>
      <div id="acc-f3q01" aria-describedby="f3q01">
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q01", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Yes</summary>
          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-info row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is an </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Employee</span></p>
              <p>The worker who is an employee would have a job description provided by the payer. </p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q01", "group": ".acc-group"}'>No </summary>
          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-primary row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Self-employed</span></p>
              <p>The worker who is self-employed would not have a job description provided by the payer. </p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>

      <p class="h5" id="f3q02">Q6. Where did the worker perform the agreed services?</p>
      <div id="acc-f3q02" aria-describedby="f3q02">
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q02", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Payer’s location</summary>
          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-info row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is an </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Employee</span></p>
              <p>The worker who is an employee generally reports to and carries out their tasks at an establishment of the payer or a location where they were assigned to work by the payer.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q02", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Worker’s location</summary>
          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-primary row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Self-employed</span></p>
              <p>The worker who is self-employed generally has more control over where they complete their work.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q02", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Client’s location</summary>

            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Facts are neutral</span></p>
              <p>The work being performed at the client’s location is not a clear sign of the worker being an employee or self-employed.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>

      <p class="h5" id="f3q03">Q7. Who determined where the worker performed the agreed service?</p>
      <div id="acc-f3q03" aria-describedby="f3q03">
        <details class="acc-group">

          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-info row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is an </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Employee</span></p>
              <p>The payer generally determines where the work of an employee is to be completed. </p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q03", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Worker </summary>
          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-primary row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Self-employed</span></p>
              <p>The worker who is self-employed can exercise more control over where they carry out their work, however, depending on the task, the payer may require the work to be performed at a particular location. </p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>

      <p class="h5" id="f3q04">Q8. Who determined when the worker performed the agreed service?</p>
      <div id="acc-f3q04" aria-describedby="f3q04">

              <p>The payer generally determines when employment duties should be completed. </p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q04", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Worker</summary>

                <span class="h3">Self-employed</span></p>
              <p>The worker has greater control over when contracted services are to be completed. </p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>

          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f3q04", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Client</summary>

      </div>
    </li>

  <li><h4 id="factor4">Remuneration</h4>
<div id="ajax-remuneration">    
    <p id="4_2">In both types of arrangements (employee or self-employed) remuneration must be paid or due to the worker. However, what the remuneration was based on, the frequency of payments, whether there were deductions withheld, and how the worker requests payments can be key indicators of either a work who is an employee or one who is self-employed. </p>
 </div>
     <p class="h5" id="f2q02">Q9. Were QPP, CPP, EI or income tax deductions withheld from the worker’s pay?</p>
      <div id="acc-f2q02" aria-describedby="f2q02">
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f2q02", "group": ".acc-group"}'>Yes</summary>
          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-info row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is an </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Employee</span></p>
              <p>The payer is required to withhold source deductions from the pay of an employee. </p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
        <details class="acc-group">
          <summary class="wb-toggle tgl-tab" data-toggle='{"parent": "#acc-f2q02", "group": ".acc-group"}'>No </summary>
          <div class="tgl-panel">
            <div tabindex="0">
              <p class="bg-primary row px-2 px-sm-3 mrgn-bttm-sm"><span class="h6">Indicator that the worker is </span><br>
                <span class="h3">Self-employed</span></p>
              <p>The worker is responsible for their own income tax deductions and QPP or CPP contributions.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>

 <p class="h5" id="f2q03">Q10. Was a pay stub given to the worker when they were paid? </p>

          </div>

            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>

            </div>
          </div>
        </details>
      </div>

   <li><h4 id="factor5">Relationship of subordination</h4>
<div id="ajax-subordination">   
   <p id="5_2">Under the <abbr title="Civil Code of Quebec">C.C.Q.</abbr>, a relationship of subordination is the main characteristic of a contract of employment. Without this relationship, there cannot be a contract of employment. If there is no subordination, it is a contract for services.</p>
<p>A relationship of subordination is the capacity, the authority, or the right of a payer to exercise control over the worker's activities and the manner in which the work is done.</p>
<p>It is the right of the payer to exercise control which is important in this review and not necessarily whether they actively use that right. It is the control of the payer over the worker that is relevant and <strong>not</strong> the control of the payer over the end result of a product or service that they have purchased.</p>

  <h3 id="step5">Do a final analysis based on all factors reviewed</h3>
 <h4 id="analyze">Analyze the facts</h4>
    <p>You need to analyze the facts by organizing and assessing the information you gathered. This process will show if the facts of the working relationship match the status the parties have chosen and are consistent with the definitions of the <abbr title="Civil Code of Quebec">C.C.Q.</abbr>. Generally, it will clarify if there is an employment relationship, or if the worker is in business on their own account.</p>

    <p>You must analyze the answer to each question from both the payer’s <strong>and</strong> the worker’s perspective to help you decide on the worker’s employment status.</p>
   
    <h4 id="weight">Weighing the facts</h4>
   <p>This is the most important part of analyzing the information. When reviewing the factors, there may be some facts that support an employee-employer relationship and other facts indicate self-employment.</p>

    <p>To help you resolve this issue, you must determine the true essence of the working relationship of the parties, comparing the intentions of the parties to the facts of their working relationship. You must review the indicators in the factors of carrying out the work, remuneration and relationship of subordination As you review each question and response, you should be able to decide what weight to assign each indicator based on a thorough review of all the responses. For example, one fact may favour an employee relationship, but another may be assigned <strong>more weight</strong> due to how the relationship is structured. This may be an indicator, because of the increased weight given to this factor, that the worker is self-employed.</p>

    <h4 id="case">Review each situation on a case-by-case basis</h4>
    <p>Whether a worker is an employee or self-employed needs to be analyzed on a case-by-case basis. Each case is unique and requires a thorough review of the facts. It is also important to remember that when attempting to decide the employment status, you must review the understanding a contract of employment and contract for services of the <abbr title="Civil Code of Quebec">C.C.Q.</abbr>.</p>
