Employment status: Employee or self-employed

This guidance (based on guide RC4110, Employee or Self-employed?) does not replace the law and does not replace a formal request for a CPP/EI ruling. No changes were made to the existing federal legislative requirements.

It is important to determine if a worker is an employee or a self-employed. Employment status has direct impacts on your reporting and withholding requirements for the worker under the Canada Pension Plan (CPP), Employment Insurance Act (EIA) and the Income Tax Act (ITA).

The analysis to determine if a worker is an employee or self-employed is also relevant to determine whether the corporation of an employee who provides services through the corporation meets the conditions for being considered a PSB (an incorporated employee) for tax purposes.
