  • New boxes added on T4 and T4A slips - Payer or employer offered dental benefits

    • Reason for change: Legislation
    • Effective date: Slips filed for calendar year 2023 and after
    • Refer to: T4 slip – Information for employers and T4A slip – Information for payers
    Brief description

    A new box 45 - Employer-offered dental benefits or box 015 - Payer-offered dental benefits must be used to indicate whether the employee/recipient or any of their family members were eligible, on December 31 of that year, to access any dental care insurance, or coverage of dental services of any kind, that an employer or payer offered.

    Publication date: October 13, 2023

  • New boxes added on T4 slip and T4 Summary - CPP2 and QPP2 contributions

    • Reason for change: Legislation
    • Effective date: Slips filed for calendar year 2024 and after
    • Refer to: T4 slip – Information for employers
    Brief description

    New boxes 16A and 17A must be used to report the amount of report the amount of the employee's second additional CPP/QPP contributions (CPP2/QPP2) the employer deducted from their employee.

    Publication date: October 13, 2023

  • New administrative policy introduced for fully remote workers - Province of employment (POE)

    • Reason for change: New CRA administrative policy
    • Effective date: Determination made after January 1, 2024
    • Refer to: Determine the province of employment (POE)
    Brief description

    Where a full-time remote work agreement was made, if an employee can be reasonably considered as attached to a particular establishment of the employer, their province of employment will be the province of that establishment.

    Publication date: October 6, 2023

  • Employers and payers can distribute specific slips by email or using security electronic portal without written consent

    • Reason for change: Legislation
    • Effective date: Effective retroactively
    • Refer to: Distribute the slips
    Brief description

    Employers and payers can distribute T4, T4A, T5 or T4FHSA slips by email or using an employer or payer's secure electronic portal without obtaining written or electronic consent from the employees or recipients before distributing the slips if all conditions are met.

    Publication date: September 5, 2023

  • Employers and payers must file electronically if they have more than 5 slips for specific information returns

    • Reason for change: Legislation
    • Effective date: Returns filed on or after January 1, 2024
    • Refer to: How to file information returns
    Brief description

    Employers and payers must file electronically if they have more than 5 slips of T4, T4A, T4A-NR or T5018 information returns of the same type for a calendar year.

    Publication date: August 12, 2023

  • Employers and payers must remit (pay) electronically if the payment is more than $10,000

    • Reason for change: Legislation
    • Effective date: Payments made after January 1, 2024
    • Refer to: How to remit (pay)
    Brief description

    Employers and payers must remit (pay) electronically if the payment is more than $10,000 and the payment is made on or after January 1, 2024.

    Publication date: July 17, 2023
