Remittance voucher booklets for accelerated remitters - PD7A-RB

On this page

  • What is a PD7A-RB
  • How often is a PD7A-RB sent
  • What to do if you received a PD7A-RB

What is a PD7A-RB

The PD7A-RB is sent to accelerated remitters who have not received a PD7A (TM). You will not receive a PD7A-RB if you remit (pay) electronically.

The PD7A-RB consists of either:

  • 27 vouchers per booklet for Threshold 1 remitters 
  • 54 vouchers per booklet for Threshold 2 remitters 

How often is a PD7A-RB sent

The PD7A-RB is sent in December.

What to do if you received a PD7A-RB

If you received a PD7A-RB, you must use the PD7A-RB to remit (pay).
