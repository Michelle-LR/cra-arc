Statement of account and partial payment for arrears balance remittance voucher - PD7D (AR)

On this page

  • What is a PD7D (AR)
  • What to do if you received a PD7D (AR)

What is a PD7D (AR)

The PD7D (AR) is sent when a partial payment for an arrears balance is received.

The PD7D (AR) consists of both:

  • A statement of account
  • A remittance voucher

What to do if you received a PD7D (AR)

If you received a PD7D (AR), you must use the PD7D (AR) to remit (pay) your arrears balance.

You cannot use the PD7D (AR) to remit current payroll remittances. You must use the PD7A for the current remittances.
