Services and information

Guidance on employment status

New - Currently in development with CVB Guidance on factors to consider to determine if a worker is an employee, self-employed or an incorporated employee, including their tax obligations under specific legislation

Open or manage an account

Determine if you need to open a payroll account and make payroll deductions depending on your status (or the worker's employment status), and the methods to use to open, modify and close an account

Set up and manage employee information

Get the employee’s social insurance number (SIN), determine the province of employment, get the completed TD1 forms before you pay an employee, determine how to increase or reduce income tax deducted at source

Calculate deductions and contributions

Learn about CPP contributions, EI premiums and income tax deductions, how to calculate the deductions on the amounts you pay, includes how to determine if you need to deduct CPP, EI and income tax from benefits and special payments
