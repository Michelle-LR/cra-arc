Correcting your tax affairs

If you believe you may be operating a personal services business and would like to correct your tax returns you previously filed, you can apply for relief under the Voluntary Disclosure Program.

Examples of non-compliance for PSB Corporations

Examples of non-compliance situation for PSB corporations:

  • Not reported non-active business income on line 207 of the T2 returns and they did not complete Schedule 7
  • Claimed the small business deduction on line 430 of the T2 return
  • Claimed other deductions that was not allowed on the T2 return
  • Claimed salary expense on the T2 return
  • Not filed T2 returns
  • Not registered for the GST/HST or filed the required returns

Learn more: Voluntary Disclosures Program – Introduction

Consequences of non-compliance
