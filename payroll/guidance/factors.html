Determine if the worker is an employee or self-employed

Content has been updated for clarity, completeness and plain language, including additional examples and legislative references.

You need to consider multiple factors to determine if the worker is an employee or is self-employed.

Unless the contract states differently, the factors are different if the contract is formed:

    • In a province not in Quebec or in a territory
    • Outside Canada and the employment is performed in a province not in Quebec, in a territory, or in another country
