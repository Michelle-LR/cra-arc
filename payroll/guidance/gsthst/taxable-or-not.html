    Important details and requirements

    Details

    Audiologists diagnose and manage hearing loss and other hearing issues.

    Audiologist must have a Master's degree to practise in their profession and the profession is regulated in every province or territory.

    Requirements

    The services must be rendered to the patient that the treatment is being paid for.

  • Exempt

    • Type of supply: Health care services
    • Type of practioners:
      • Registered nurse
      • Registered nursing assistant
      • Registered practical nurse
      • Registered psychiatric nurse
    Important details and requirements

    Details

    Nursing involves a combination of assessing the health care needs of a patient, determining the appropriate treatment or equipment, providing supportive, preventative, therapeutic, palliative and rehabilitative treatments and administering medications.

    Nurses must have a Master's degree to practise in their profession and the profession is regulated in every province or territory.

    Requirements

    The nursing services must be rendered by a nurse to a patient within a nurse-patient relationship, regardless of the location.

    A nursing service can be rendered in a hospital, a patient's home or any other location.

    ETA: Schedule V, Part II, section 6
