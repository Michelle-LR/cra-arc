<p class="h3 mrgn-tp-0">Category of supply: Real property</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled mrgn-bttm-md colcount-sm-2"> 
<li><strong>Type of supply:</strong> Sale or lease of commercial real property</li> 
<li><strong>Business activity:</strong> Most businesses</li> 
</ul> 
<details> <summary>Explanation</summary>
<p>The sale or lease of commercial real property is taxable in most common situations. </p>
<p>A leasehold interest or the assignment of a commercial lease is considered a sale of commercial real property and is taxable at the applicable rate.</p>
<h4>Remitting the GST/HST</h4>
<p>The recipient of a taxable sale of real property is required to remit the GST/HST directly to the Receiver General for Canada when one of the following applies:</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or cnjnctn-sm">
  
<li class="cnjnctn-col">The supplier is a non-resident</li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Non-residential real property is sold to a GST/HST registrant</li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col">Residential real property is sold to a GST/HST registration that is not an individual</li>
</ul>
</details>
<p class="bg-warning"><strong>Tax status: </strong> Taxable at 5%, 13% or 15%</p> 
<p class="mrgn-bttm-md text-muted text-right small">ETA: Subsections 136(1) and 221(2), Schedule V, Part I, subsection 9(2)</p> 
</li>

<li data-wb-tags= "supply-9 supply-9a supply-9b business-1" class="well brdr-rds-0">
<p class="h3 mrgn-tp-0">Category of supply: Real property</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled mrgn-bttm-md colcount-sm-2"> 
<li><strong>Type of supply:</strong> Sale or lease of a parking space  </li> 
<li><strong>Business activity:</strong> Most businesses</li> 
</ul> 
<details> <summary>Requirements</summary>
<h4>Sale</h4>
<p>The sale of a parking space is exempt when it was used for personal use and enjoyment, and is sold together with the exempt sale of residential housing or with a unit in a residential condominium complex.</p>
<h4>Lease</h4>
<p>The lease of a parking space throughout a period of at least one month to a lessee of a residential unit is exempt if one of the following applies:</p>
<ul class="list-unstyled cnjnctn-type-or cnjnctn-sm">
  
<li class="cnjnctn-col">The parking space forms part of the residential complex or trailer park</li>
<li class="cnjnctn-col">The parking space is supplied by the owner as incidental to the use and enjoyment of the complex, unit or site of the unit as a place of residence</li>
</ul>
</details>
<p class="bg-success"><strong>Tax status if all requirements are met:</strong> Exempt</p> 
<p class="mrgn-bttm-md text-muted text-right small">ETA: Schedule V, Part I, sections 8 and 8.1</p> 
</li>
