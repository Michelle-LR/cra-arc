Determine the employment status: Employee, self-employed or incorporated employee

It is important to determine if a worker is an employee, self-employed or an incorporated employee. Employment status has direct impacts on how a worker is treated under specific legislation such as the Canada Pension Plan, Employment Insurance Act and the Income Tax Act.
