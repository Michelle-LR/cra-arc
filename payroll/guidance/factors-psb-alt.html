Determine if the worker is an incorporated employee

This information does not replace the law and does not replace a formal request for a CPP/EI ruling. No changes were made to the existing federal legislative requirements.

Under the ITA, the corporation is considered a PSB if all of the following conditions apply:

  • What is a related person and a specified shareholder

    Related person

    A trust, individual, corporation, partnership or a succession, or a body that is an association, club, commission, union or another organization of any kind.

    Specified shareholder

    A specified shareholder of the corporation is an individual who directly or indirectly owns at least 10% of the issued shares of any class of a capital stock of the corporation or a related corporation.

The corporation must meet all conditions above to be considered a PSB

The worker is not an incorporated employee and is not operating a PSB.
