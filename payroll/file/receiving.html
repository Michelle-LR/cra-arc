Receiving payroll correspondence from the CRA

Once the CRA receives your information returns, they will send you correspondence if either:

  • The CRA owes you a refund or rebate
  • Your amount owing is more than the payment you made

The CRA may also send you other types of correspondence (for example, a Pensionable and Insurable Earnings Review (PIER) notice) depending on the situation.

Types

  • Notice to file - TX14PD
  • Pensionable and insurable earnings review (PIER)
  • Tax deduction, CPP and EI discrepancy notice - PD4R
  • Notice of assessment - PD7D
