Claim a rebate

General GST/HST rebates are claimed under one of several reason codes by filling out Form GST189, General Application for GST/HST Rebate. You can only use one reason code per rebate application. If you are eligible to claim a rebate under more than one code, use a separate rebate application for each reason code.
