[Managing your taxes when in an abusive or violent situation]

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) understands the importance of getting benefit and credit payments, especially during times of stress and transition.

If you are facing an abusive or violet situation, including economic abuse, we are commited to helping you continue to get support.

You will never need to contact an abusive person or organization to provide any information to the CRA, use your My Account, or apply for and get your benefits.
