Corporate federal tax credits

Corporations may be eligible to claim the following federal tax credits when filing their T2 Corporation Income Tax Return:

Income lines for the Final Return
Line number Tax credit
616 Manufacturing and processing profits deduction and zero-emission technology manufacturing deduction
11300 Old Age Security pension
11400 CPP or QPP benefits
11500 Other pensions or superannuation
11600 Elected split-pension amount
11900 Employment insurance benefits
11905 Employment insurance maternity and parental benefits and provincial parental insurance plan benefits
12000 to 12100 Investment income, such as dividends and interest
12500 Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP)
12599 to 12600 Rental income
