If you are facing an abusive or violet situation, including economic abuse, the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) is commited to helping you continue to get support.

You will never need to contact an abusive person or organization to provide any information to the CRA, use your My Account, or apply for and get your benefits.

Updating your personal information

What to do when the CRA contacts you

You may get a letter from the CRA asking you to confirm your personal information or provide supporting documentation.

Send any supporting information back to the address listed on the letter or through your CRA My Account.

If your situation prevents you from getting the requested documents or information, you can send any of the following explaining your situation:

  • A letter from a trusted third party representative such as:
    • a band council
    • a shelter
    • a resettlement office
    • a member of the clergy
  • A copy of a restraining order or an order of protection
  • A copy of a police report

If you have questions about the documents requested, or about benefit and credit payments, you can contact us.

Register for email notifications from the CRA. We may send you notifications about your account by email. Make sure your information is up-to-date.
