Find out if a supply is taxable, zero-rated or exempt

You need to determine if a supply is taxable, zero-rated or exempt. Whether to charge the GST/HST may depend on who the supply is made to and who makes the supply.

Generally, every sale, lease or other supply made in Canada is GST/HST taxable, unless it is:

  • Zero-rated (taxable at 0%)
  • GST/HST exempt
